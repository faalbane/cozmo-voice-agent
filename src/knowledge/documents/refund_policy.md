# ShopEase Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Policy
All purchases from ShopEase are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of delivery. Items must be returned in their original condition with all tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

## How to Request a Refund
Customers can request a refund by contacting our support team via phone or email. A return shipping label will be provided free of charge for all eligible returns. Simply pack the item in its original packaging and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

## Exceptions
The following items are not eligible for refund:
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Perishable goods (food, flowers)
- Digital downloads and gift cards
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)

## Late Returns (31-60 Days)
Items returned between 31 and 60 days after delivery are eligible for store credit only, not a cash refund. The store credit is valid for 12 months from the date of issue.

## Damaged or Defective Items
If you received a damaged or defective item, contact us immediately. We will provide a full refund or replacement regardless of the return window. Photo evidence of the damage is required for processing.

## Refund Methods
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear on your statement. For orders paid with gift cards, the refund is issued as store credit.
