# ShopEase Objection Handling Guide

## Customer Wants to Cancel Order
When a customer requests order cancellation:
- Orders that haven't shipped yet can be cancelled immediately with a full refund.
- Orders already shipped must go through the return process after delivery.
- For subscription cancellations, offer a 20% discount on the next 3 months before processing.
- If the customer insists on cancellation, process it gracefully and let them know they're welcome back anytime.

## Customer Unhappy with Product Quality
- Apologize sincerely and ask for specific details about the issue.
- Offer an immediate replacement or full refund — customer's choice.
- For high-value items (over $100), offer a 15% discount on their next purchase as a goodwill gesture.
- Log the quality complaint for our product team to review.

## Customer Complains About Shipping Delays
- Acknowledge the frustration and check the current tracking status.
- Standard shipping is 5-7 business days. Express shipping is 2-3 business days.
- If an order is more than 2 days past its estimated delivery date, offer free upgrade to express shipping on their next order.
- If the package appears lost (no tracking updates for 5+ days), initiate a replacement shipment immediately.

## Customer Disputes a Charge
- Verify the charge details with the customer (date, amount, last 4 digits of card).
- Check the order history to confirm whether the charge matches a legitimate order.
- If the charge is unauthorized, escalate to the fraud prevention team immediately.
- If it's a duplicate charge, initiate a refund for the duplicate and apologize.

## Customer Wants a Price Adjustment
- Our price match guarantee covers 14 days from purchase.
- The competing price must be from an authorized retailer (not marketplace sellers).
- Process the refund of the price difference to the original payment method.
- If outside the 14-day window, offer a 10% discount code for their next purchase as a courtesy.

## Shipping Information
- Standard shipping: $5.99, arrives in 5-7 business days.
- Express shipping: $12.99, arrives in 2-3 business days.
- Free standard shipping on orders over $50.
- ShopEase Rewards Gold members get free express shipping on all orders.
- We ship to all 50 US states and select international destinations.
- International shipping rates vary by destination, starting at $15.99.
