# ShopEase Product Information

## Product Categories
ShopEase offers products across several categories:
- **Electronics**: Smartphones, laptops, tablets, headphones, smart home devices
- **Home & Kitchen**: Furniture, cookware, bedding, home decor, storage solutions
- **Fashion**: Clothing, shoes, accessories, jewelry for men, women, and children
- **Sports & Outdoors**: Fitness equipment, camping gear, sports apparel, bicycles
- **Beauty & Health**: Skincare, haircare, vitamins, wellness products

## Pricing
All prices on ShopEase include applicable taxes. We offer a price match guarantee — if you find the same item at a lower price from an authorized retailer within 14 days of purchase, we'll refund the difference. Bulk discounts are available for orders of 10 or more identical items, typically 15% off.

## Product Warranty
All electronics come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty plans are available for purchase at checkout, covering an additional 1-3 years. The extended warranty covers defects, accidental damage, and battery degradation.

## Loyalty Program — ShopEase Rewards
Members earn 1 point per dollar spent. Points can be redeemed at a rate of 100 points = $5 discount. Gold members (spending $500+ per year) earn 1.5 points per dollar and get free expedited shipping on all orders.

## Gift Cards
ShopEase gift cards are available in denominations from $10 to $500. Gift cards never expire and can be used for any purchase on our platform. They cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by law.
